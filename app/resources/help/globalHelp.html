Global search page contains 8 fields that can be used as criteria for your search:
  • Start date: conversation start date and time
  • End date: conversation end date and time
  • Sort By: how to sort the results
  • Flagging rule: custom flagging rule
  • Custodian: Select a custodian to search for
  • Country / LE: The country or Legal entity to filter by
  • Keywords: Text to search for in the title and metadata
  • Flagging rule: Text to search for in the content
There are also source specific checkboxes to filter by content source. Choose as many as you need
After typing and selecting the search criteria you just need to click the Search button located under the form.
