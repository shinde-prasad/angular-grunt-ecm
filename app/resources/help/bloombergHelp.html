Bloomberg search page contains 3 fields that can be used as criteria for your search:
  • Start date: conversation start date and time
  • End date: conversation end date and time
  • Flagging rule: custom flagging rule
After typing and selecting the search criteria you just need to click the Search button located under the form.

Additionally you can click the "Instant Bloomberg" checkbox and add 7 more filters
  • Phone ID: Dealer Board phone ID
  • Custodian: custodians associated with the conversation
  • Trader ID: conversation trader identifier
  • Flagging rule: custom flagging rule
Also you can click the "Bloomberg Messaging" checkbox and add 4 more filters
  • Keywords: keywords associated with the email
  • Custodian: custodians associated with the email
  • Flagging rule: custom flagging rule
  • Content: message content
