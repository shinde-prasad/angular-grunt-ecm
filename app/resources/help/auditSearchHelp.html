Audit search allows you to look for actions performed by your compliance team. This form contains 5 fields that can be used as criteria for your search:
  • Start date: action start date
  • End date: action end date
  • User: username that performed the action
  • Action: the action performed by the user
  • Resource: the different resources available in the application
After typing and selecting the search criteria you just need to click the Search button located under the form.
